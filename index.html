Why We Chose GIS: Making Planting Smarter and More Sustainable

Planting and gardening are more than just putting seeds in the ground—they require careful planning to make sure plants grow healthy and strong. Using a GIS (Geographic Information System) allows us to analyze sunlight, soil types, and space to make smarter planting decisions.

Simulated GIS Tool

Our Virtual GIS Plant Finder is an interactive tool that simulates how a real GIS system works for planting. You can explore which plants grow best based on conditions like water type, soil moisture, sunlight, and space.

Although it’s a mock-up version, it helps you visualize, plan, and experiment with planting layouts—making gardening smarter and more fun!
