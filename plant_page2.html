01

About these plants

Plants in this category need full sunlight but also a moderate, consistently moist soil, often rich in organic matter. Loamy soils provide excellent drainage and nutrient availability, making them ideal for flowering plants, fruit trees, and shrubs. These plants grow faster and appear lush and vibrant when they receive both sun and moisture consistently.

02

Impacts on Parks

Integrating these plants into Myanmar's local parks helps create shaded picnic areas, flower beds, and visually appealing green spaces while supporting local biodiversity. They also stabilize the soil and reduce erosion in open areas. You can mostly find them in fertile river valleys like the Ayeyarwady Basin and around the hilly regions of Shan State, where the soil is naturally loamy and water is available throughout the year.
