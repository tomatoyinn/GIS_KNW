01

About These Plants

These plants prefer areas with some sunlight but can also tolerate partial shade. They thrive in loamy soils that retain moderate moisture without becoming waterlogged. Many ornamental plants, shrubs, and small trees fall into this category, offering a balance between shade-loving and sun-loving species. Their moderate water requirements make them manageable for park maintenance.

02

Impacts on Parks

For Myanmar’s parks, these plants are excellent for creating shaded walking trails, understorey layers beneath larger trees, or mixed flowerbeds. They help maintain soil fertility and provide habitats for birds and insects. They are commonly found in central Myanmar’s semi-shaded areas, hilly foothills, and around towns with gardened spaces, where loamy soil and moderate rainfall support their growth.
