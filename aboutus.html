Many visitors and local residents are unaware of how plants grow in different zones of a park, which areas need more care, and how to protect the environment responsibly. By creating this website, we want to simplify the process of learning about plants, promote eco-friendly actions, and support the gardeners who maintain these green spaces

  • Help visitors learn more about plants
  • Encourage sustainable habits
  • Support park gardeners
  • Involve the community

  • Add more plants and environmental data to the website
  • Update the interactive park map with new features
  • Include interviews with gardeners for authentic insights
  • Improve website interactivity, including interactive maps, plant info, and visual guides

  • Our website was created in 2025 by Year 9 Sangria, Group 3, to help improve local parks using GIS technology. We wanted to show how mapping environmental data can guide planting and promote sustainable habits.
  • Through park visits, gardener interviews, and research, we developed this interactive platform to educate visitors, share plant information, and encourage the community to help make parks greener and healthier.
